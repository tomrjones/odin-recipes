Red Beans and Rice with Sausage

This is my favorite meal to make when needing to feed a large group of people

Description

This recipe may take some time to finish but luckily for us the pot does all of the hard work. Overall prep is fairly simple and the results will blow your family's minds.

Ingredients

  • 2 pound dry kidney beans
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon dried sage
  • 2 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups long grain white rice

You will need to do a little prep the night before with the beans. Make sure you read ahead and are aware of this

Step by Step Guide

  1. Rinse beans, and then soak in a large pot of water overnight.
  2. The next day when you are ready to begin gather all ingredients
  3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic in olive oil for 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Rinse beans, and transfer to a large pot with 8 cups water. Stir cooked vegetables into beans. Season with bay leaves, parsley, thyme, Cajun seasoning, cayenne pepper, and sage. Bring to a boil, and then reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 2 1/2 hours.
  5. Stir sausage into beans, and continue to simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, prepare the rice. Bring water and rice to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Once the rice is done you can serve the red beans over the rice. Keeping these two seperate allows people to get their desired consistency of rice vs beans.
  8. Enjoy the fruits of your labor!
